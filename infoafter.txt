Post-install notes:

    1.  Add APPDIR/bin to your PATH.

    2.  Add APPDIR/data/schema/cim214 to your CIMPLE_MOF_PATH 
        (or whichever schema directory you prefer).

    3.  CIMPLE was installed into the following locations:

            APPDIR/bin - CIMPLE binaries
            APPDIR/lib - CIMPLE libraries
            APPDIR/include/cimple - CIMPLE include files.
            APPDIR/data/schema - CIM schemas (MOF files).
